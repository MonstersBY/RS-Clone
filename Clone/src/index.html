What is Colonist?

Short description: Colonist.io is a web game alternative to the top selling board game Settlers of Catan

Genres: Multiplayer, Board Game, Strategy, IO Game, Puzzle, Social

Platform: HTML5, PC browser, Tablet browser

Long description: Colonist.io is a web based, free online alternative to the challenging social and strategy based board game Settlers of Catan. You take on the role of a Colonist by building settlements and expanding your territory. Colonist combines extremely simple rules with tremendously deep gameplay. Use strategy, planning, and cunning negotiation to win. Base game is 4 players. The first player to reach 10 points wins. Can be played solo versus bots, with friends or with online players all over the world.
